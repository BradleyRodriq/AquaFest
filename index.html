AquaFest 2025

Clean Waters and Healthy Communities Festival

United for the Prevention of Aquatic Pollution

Date: Saturday, November 15, 2025

Location: Aguirre, Salinas, Puerto Rico

RSVP
Follow us: Facebook Instagram

AquaFest is a key initiative within the NOAA Marine Debris Program's 2023–2028 Strategic Plan to Reduce Aquatic Debris in Puerto Rico. This annual festival brings together local organizations, community members, and stakeholders through informational booths, workshops, and interactive activities—raising awareness about aquatic debris and promoting sustainable practices to protect our marine environments.

Objectives

  • Increase Awareness of Aquatic Pollution

    Educate participants about the sources, impacts, and solutions related to marine and aquatic debris through workshops, exhibits, and interactive activities.

  • Encourage Community Participation

    Recruit and involve community members in hands-on activities such as beach cleanups, recycling workshops, and art projects using marine debris.

  • Promote Sustainable Practices

    Demonstrate practical solutions for waste management and pollution reduction, including reuse, recycling, and waste minimization.

  • Encourage Collaboration

    Facilitate collaboration and networking among local organizations, businesses, and government agencies involved in marine conservation.
